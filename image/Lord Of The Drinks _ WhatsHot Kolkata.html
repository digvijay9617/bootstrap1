Lord Of The Drinks Has Finally Opened In Kolkata & It Has The Tallest Bar Ever!

1 of 10

What's Hot About It?

The 11th edition of the largest restaurant chain in India, Lord of the Drink nestles in the heart of Kolkata in a sought after mall, The South City Mall. The Lord has bestowed Kolkata with the tallest bar ever, a 40 ft high double-height bar with an upper stage for the bartenders and lower stage for you to enjoy their sipping sessions.

It showcases a strong influence drawn from the neo-classical and modern era with customised brass disc chandeliers. Revolutionising the very essence of nightlife in Kolkata, Lord of the Drinks is a huge shift from the usual resto-bars in town due to the subtle play of levels and heights. Usage of motifs, mirrors and 3D interiors with a touch of gold, the outlet follows a robust combination of green and cherry complemented with a very unique collection of Chairs to make you feel the lavish vibes.

Coming to their food, you will be delighted to binge on their delectable Continental, Chinese, European, North Indian dishes along with lots of finger food. You will definitely love their Crispy Corn Bhel, Thai Cottage Cheese Spring Rolls, Belgian Pork Ribs, Tandoori Chicken and Cheese Balls and Beer Chicken chips among their starters. From the rest of their menu, the Mustard Crusted Fish Tikka, Pil Pil Prawns, Chicken Gyoza, LOTD Tandoori Chicken, Lord of the Meat pizza and Mutton Rogan Josh.

Now comes their bar! Lord of the Drinks shakes up the finest concoctions of the city, chilling up your party with their in-house quirky and ecstatic drinks. Among their signature which are a must-try, you will find Purple Rain, My 18th Birthday, Lavender Sour, Ginger Highball, Barrel Aged Negroni and Kanpai Mojito to be all-time delights. Apart from Whiskey Sour, Bloody Mary, Penicillin, Bronx and Daiquiri, you will also find varieties of Sangrias, Whiskies, Wine, Draught, Domestic and Imported Beer, Vodka, Rum, Gin, Cognac, Aperitif as well as their booze-free concoctions to be perfect for your after-meal chills.
